What Is QuickBooks ProAdvisor?
------------------------------

QuickBooks ProAdvisor is a certification program by Intuit that recognizes professionals who demonstrate expertise in QuickBooks products. Certified ProAdvisors help businesses with setup, bookkeeping, reporting, troubleshooting, and optimization of QuickBooks software.

Create an Intuit Account and Enroll

To begin, create an Intuit account and enroll in the ProAdvisor program. Enrollment is free and provides access to training materials, practice tools, and certification exams.

Pass the ProAdvisor Certification Exam
--------------------------------------

After completing training, you must pass the certification exam to earn the QuickBooks ProAdvisor credential. The exam tests your knowledge of QuickBooks Online features, workflows, and best practices.

Once certified, your profile becomes visible to businesses searching for QuickBooks experts.
