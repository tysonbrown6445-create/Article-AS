Overview of QuickBooks Self-Employed
------------------------------------

QuickBooks Self-Employed focuses on basic bookkeeping and tax tracking, making it ideal for individuals who work for themselves. It helps separate business and personal finances while keeping everything organized in one place.

Users can securely access their account through QuickBooks Self-Employed Online Login from any device with an internet connection.

Key Features Included in QuickBooks Self-Employed
-------------------------------------------------

### 1\. Income and Expense Tracking

QuickBooks Self-Employed automatically tracks your income and expenses by connecting to your bank and credit card accounts. Transactions are categorized to give you a clear view of your cash flow.

### 2\. Mileage Tracking

The software includes built-in mileage tracking using your mobile device’s GPS. This helps maximize tax deductions by accurately recording business travel.

### 3\. Estimated Quarterly Taxes

One of the most valuable features of QuickBooks Self-Employed is automatic quarterly tax estimation. The software calculates your estimated taxes based on income and expenses, helping you avoid surprises during tax season.

### 4\. Receipt Capture

You can snap photos of receipts using your phone, and QuickBooks Self-Employed will store and organize them digitally for easy reference.

### 5\. Simple Invoicing

Create and send professional invoices, track payments, and monitor outstanding balances—all from one dashboard.
