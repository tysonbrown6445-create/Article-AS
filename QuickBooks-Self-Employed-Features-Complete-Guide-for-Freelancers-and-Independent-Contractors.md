What Is QuickBooks Self-Employed?
---------------------------------

QuickBooks Self-Employed is an accounting solution built for freelancers, independent contractors, and sole proprietors. It focuses on essential bookkeeping and tax-related features that help users stay organized and compliant throughout the year.

You can securely access your account anytime through the **QuickBooks Self-Employed Online Login**, making it easy to manage finances on the go.

Top Features of QuickBooks Self-Employed
----------------------------------------

### 1\. Automatic Income and Expense Tracking

QuickBooks Self-Employed automatically imports transactions from connected bank accounts and credit cards. It categorizes income and expenses, reducing manual data entry and helping you maintain accurate records.

### 2\. Expense Categorization for Tax Deductions

The software separates business and personal expenses, ensuring only eligible costs are counted as deductions—an essential feature during tax season.

### 3\. Mileage Tracking

One of the most valuable QuickBooks Self-Employed features is automatic mileage tracking. The app tracks business miles using GPS and calculates deductions accurately.

### 4\. Quarterly Estimated Tax Calculation

QuickBooks estimates quarterly taxes based on your real-time income and expenses, helping you plan ahead and avoid penalties.

### 5\. Simple Invoicing

Create and send professional invoices directly from the platform and track payments without switching tools.
