What Is QuickBooks Self-Employed?
---------------------------------

QuickBooks Self-Employed is an accounting software created specifically for freelancers and self-employed professionals. It focuses on income tracking, expense categorization, mileage tracking, and tax estimation, without the complexity of full-scale accounting systems.

Users can easily access their account using the QuickBooks Self-Employed Online Login, making it convenient to manage finances from anywhere.

Key Features of QuickBooks Self-Employed
----------------------------------------

### 1\. Income and Expense Tracking

QuickBooks Self-Employed automatically imports transactions from linked bank accounts and credit cards. It helps users categorize expenses accurately and maintain clean financial records.

### 2\. Tax Estimation and Deductions

One standout feature highlighted in many QuickBooks Self-Employed reviews is real-time tax estimation. The software calculates quarterly estimated taxes and identifies deductible expenses to help avoid surprises during tax season.

### 3\. Mileage Tracking

The built-in mileage tracker uses GPS to log business miles automatically, ensuring accurate deductions with minimal effort.

### 4\. Simple Invoicing

Users can create and send professional invoices, track payments, and monitor outstanding balances directly from the dashboard.
