What Is QuickBooks Self-Employed? A Complete Guide for Freelancers and Contractors
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you’re a freelancer, independent contractor, or sole proprietor, you may be wondering what QuickBooks Self-Employed is and whether it’s the right accounting tool for your business. QuickBooks Self-Employed is a simple, cloud-based solution designed specifically for individuals who work for themselves and need an easy way to track income, expenses, and taxes.

This article explains what QuickBooks Self-Employed does, who it’s for, and how it helps self-employed professionals manage finances efficiently.

What Is QuickBooks Self-Employed?
---------------------------------

QuickBooks Self-Employed is an accounting and tax management software created by Intuit for people who don’t have employees and don’t need full payroll services. It focuses on:

*   Tracking business income
    
*   Categorizing expenses
    
*   Estimating quarterly taxes
    
*   Separating personal and business finances
    

Unlike traditional accounting software, it’s built for simplicity and ease of use.

Who Should Use QuickBooks Self-Employed?
----------------------------------------

QuickBooks Self-Employed is ideal for:

*   Freelancers
    
*   Independent contractors
    
*   Gig workers (rideshare, delivery, creators)
    
*   Consultants and solopreneurs
    

If you don’t run payroll or manage inventory, this tool offers everything you need without unnecessary complexity.

Built-in mileage tracking and receipt capture help maximize deductions and keep records organized.
