How to Get a QuickBooks ProAdvisor Certification Training?
----------------------------------------------------------

If you’re an accountant, bookkeeper, or financial professional looking to advance your career, learning how to get a QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification training is a smart move. The QuickBooks ProAdvisor program offers free training, certifications, and tools that help you build expertise, gain client trust, and grow your practice.

This guide explains where to find ProAdvisor training, how it works, and how to prepare effectively for certification.

What Is QuickBooks ProAdvisor Certification Training?
-----------------------------------------------------

[QuickBooks ProAdvisor](https://www.wizxpert.com/find-a-quickbooks-proadvisor/) certification training is a structured learning program provided by Intuit. It helps professionals master QuickBooks Online features, workflows, and best practices so they can confidently support clients and businesses.

The training is self-paced, beginner-friendly, and updated regularly to match the latest QuickBooks changes.

Create a QuickBooks Online Accountant Account
---------------------------------------------

To access ProAdvisor training, you must first sign up for a QuickBooks Online Accountant account. This is free and required for certification.

Once registered, you can access the dashboard using the [Intuit QuickBooks Online login](https://www.wizxpert.com/quickbooks-online-login-problems/), where all training materials and exams are available.

Enroll in QuickBooks ProAdvisor Training
----------------------------------------

After logging in:

1.  Go to the ProAdvisor tab
    
2.  Choose QuickBooks Online Certification
    
3.  Start the training modules
    

The training includes videos, tutorials, real-world scenarios, and practice questions designed to prepare you for the exam.

Practice with the QuickBooks Online Test Drive
----------------------------------------------

Hands-on experience is key to passing the certification exam. You can practice without risk by using the [QuickBooks Online test drive link](https://www.wizxpert.com/qbo-test-drive/), which provides access to a sample company.

This lets you explore features such as:

*   Invoicing and expenses
    
*   Reports and dashboards
    
*   Bank feeds and reconciliation
    

Practicing in the test drive environment builds confidence and practical skills.

Understand Different Client Needs
---------------------------------

As part of your training, you’ll learn how to support different types of clients, including freelancers and contractors. Knowing how the [QuickBooks Self-Employed Online Login](https://www.wizxpert.com/quickbooks-self-employed-login/) works helps you recommend the right tools and workflows for self-employed clients.

This knowledge is especially useful for exam questions and real-world client scenarios.

Prepare for the Certification Exam
----------------------------------

Before taking the exam:

*   Complete all training modules
    
*   Review sample questions
    
*   Practice regularly in the test drive company
    

The exam is open-book, timed, and can be retaken if needed. Staying consistent with practice greatly improves your chances of passing on the first attempt.

Maintain Technical Knowledge
----------------------------

ProAdvisors who support desktop or hybrid environments should also understand system maintenance tasks, such as how to [upgrade QuickBooks Database Server Manager](https://www.wizxpert.com/quickbooks-database-server-manager/). This knowledge helps prevent performance issues and strengthens your overall expertise, even if your primary focus is QuickBooks Online.

How Long Does ProAdvisor Training Take?
---------------------------------------

Most professionals complete QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification training within:

*   **10–20 hours** for beginners
    
*   **5–10 hours** for those with prior QuickBooks experience
    

You can learn at your own pace and pause or resume anytime.
